Searh For An Adventure.

Use our search to decide what type of adventure, the location, cost range, when, number of people, and options you're looking for.

Request Booking & Setup Payment.

Once you've found the right adventure, submit a request to the host. Also make sure your payment is on file, when the hosts confirm you will be billed.

Recieve Confirmation

Work out the details with the host and make sure you have all of the required equipment, skills, & prepare for your adventure.

Go On Your Adventure. Review & Share.

Enjoy your experience, rate your host, and share with the community.
